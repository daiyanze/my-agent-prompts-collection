# Analysis & Research Prompts

Use these prompts to ask an AI agent to analyze data, documents, or topics in a structured way.

---

## Analyze a Dataset or Table

```
Please analyze the following data and provide:
1. A brief summary of what the data represents
2. Key statistics or patterns (trends, outliers, distributions)
3. Interesting or unexpected findings
4. Any data quality issues you notice (missing values, inconsistencies)
5. Suggested next steps or deeper analyses worth pursuing

<data>
[PASTE DATA / TABLE / CSV HERE]
</data>
```

---

## Compare Options

```
I need to choose between the following options. Please compare them across the dimensions I care about and give me a clear recommendation with reasoning.

**Context / goal:** [DESCRIBE WHAT YOU ARE TRYING TO ACHIEVE]

**Options:**
1. [OPTION A]
2. [OPTION B]
3. [OPTION C — add more as needed]

**Dimensions to compare:** [e.g. cost, performance, ease of maintenance, scalability — or leave blank for the agent to determine relevant dimensions]

End with a recommendation and explain which trade-offs were most important to your conclusion.
```

---

## Pros & Cons Analysis

```
List the pros and cons of [TOPIC / DECISION / APPROACH]. Be balanced and thorough. After the list, provide a brief overall assessment.
```

---

## Root Cause Analysis

```
Something went wrong: [DESCRIBE THE PROBLEM / INCIDENT].

Please help me conduct a root cause analysis:
1. What are the possible contributing factors?
2. Which is the most likely root cause, and why?
3. What evidence would confirm or rule out each factor?
4. What immediate mitigation steps should be taken?
5. What longer-term changes would prevent recurrence?
```

---

## Summarize a Research Paper / Article

```
Please summarize the following research paper / article. Include:
- The core research question or problem
- The methodology used
- Key findings and results
- Limitations acknowledged by the authors
- Practical implications or takeaways

<text>
[PASTE PAPER / ARTICLE TEXT HERE]
</text>
```

---

## SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)

```
Conduct a SWOT analysis for [SUBJECT, e.g. "launching a new product feature", "adopting technology X", or "this business strategy"]:

- **Strengths:** Internal advantages
- **Weaknesses:** Internal disadvantages
- **Opportunities:** External factors to leverage
- **Threats:** External risks to mitigate

After the SWOT table, provide a one-paragraph strategic summary.
```

---

## Fact-Check a Claim

```
Please evaluate the following claim for accuracy:

**Claim:** [STATE THE CLAIM]

1. Is this claim accurate, partially accurate, or inaccurate based on your knowledge?
2. What evidence supports or contradicts it?
3. What important nuances or context is missing?
4. What would a reliable source look like for verifying this?

Be explicit about your confidence level and any limitations in your knowledge.
```
