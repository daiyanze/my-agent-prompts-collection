# my-agent-prompts-collection

A curated collection of reusable prompts to help you (and everyone else) get better, more reliable results from AI agents and assistants.

The prompts are designed to be **copy-paste ready** — just fill in the `[PLACEHOLDERS]` and go.

---

## Why this collection?

AI agents are only as good as the instructions you give them. The right prompt structure can turn a vague, mediocre response into a thorough, actionable one. This collection captures battle-tested prompt patterns across the most common use cases so you don't have to reinvent the wheel every time.

## How to use

1. **Browse** the category that matches your need.
2. **Copy** the relevant prompt template.
3. **Fill in** the `[PLACEHOLDERS]` with your specific details.
4. **Paste** into your AI agent (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, etc.).

> **Tip:** The general prompts in the [⚡ General](prompts/general/better-responses.md) section can be combined with any other prompt to improve the quality of the response - for example, appending *"Give me 3 options"* or *"Challenge my assumptions"* to a coding or planning prompt.
